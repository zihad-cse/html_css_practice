Sticky Position

An element with sticky position is positioned based on the user's scroll position. A sticky element toggles between relative and fixed , depending on the scroll position. It is positioned relative until a given offset position is met in the viewport - then it "sticks" in place (like position:fixed).


The red box has the sticky position, But when scrolled down the box will stick to the top of the page when met with the given offset position
